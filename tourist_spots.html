Tourist spots in Bangladesh

Bangladesh is home to a variety of captivating tourist spots that highlight its natural beauty and cultural heritage. Cox's Bazar, the world's longest natural sea beach, is a popular destination for beach lovers. The Sundarbans, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, offers a unique experience with its vast mangrove forests and endangered Bengal tigers. Srimangal, known for its lush tea gardens and serene landscapes, is perfect for nature enthusiasts. The historic Somapura Mahavihara, one of the largest ancient Buddhist monasteries, and the ancient mosque city of Bagerhat are significant cultural and architectural sites. These spots reflect Bangladesh's diversity and charm.
